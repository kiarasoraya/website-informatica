Stadia

The World Cup will be held in eight different venues across Qatar. These venues are:

  • Al Bayt Stadium
  • Ahmad Bin Ali Stadium
  • Al Janoub Stadium
  • Al Thumama Stadium
  • Education City Stadium
  • Khalifa International Stadium
  • Stadium 974
  • Lusail Iconic Stadium
